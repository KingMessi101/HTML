Cuisines from all around the world!

Dosa (South India)

A dosa is a thin, savoury crepe in Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black gram and rice. Dosas are served hot, often with chutney and sambar. The dosa originated in South India, but its precise geographical origins are unknown!

Sushi (Japan)

Sushi is a Japanese dish that consists of specially prepared rice and usually fish or seafood, often raw. The rice is mixed with vinegar, salt, and sugar. Sushi can also have vegetables or other ingredients, and can be wrapped in seaweed or served in different shapes and forms!

Pasta (Italy)

Pasta is a food made from flour, starch and water. It is usually cooked in hot water before being eaten. Pasta has been very popular in Italy and has also been eaten in many parts of Asia for a long time. Pasta has been devoloped by many countries and has become a super delisious dish!
